Vocabulary Box

Pelajari kosakata penting di bawah ini yang sering digunakan oleh seorang *Content Creator* saat mengulas produk digital.

review Verb

Arti: mengulas / mengkaji

Ex: "Today, I want to review this new phone."

useful Adjective

Arti: berguna / bermanfaat

Ex: "This tablet is very useful for students."

budget-friendly Adjective

Arti: ramah di kantong / murah

Ex: "Ring-lights are budget-friendly tools."

downside Noun

Arti: kekurangan / sisi negatif

Ex: "The only downside is the short cable."

durable Adjective

Arti: awet / tahan lama

Ex: "The phone case is strong and durable."

Reading Task: Comprehension Check

Read the official script carefully to analyze the product details, then answer the questions below.

Official Video Script: "$400 versus $1,200 Samsung tablet. How similar are they? The S9 FE has normal tablet proportions, but the S9 Ultra is completely insane. It's super thin and extremely wide. It feels almost wrong, like I ripped off a laptop screen. Both come with an S Pen that's color matched, and while the Ultra just comes in graphite and beige, the FE actually has a lavender option and this cute mint color. The FE is slower, but outside of games, it's not that noticeable. However, you can definitely tell these two displays apart. The FE has a 90Hz LCD display that's impressive for its price, but it's no match for the Ultra's very extra 120Hz OLED screen."

Reading Task Answer Key:

A. Samsung Galaxy S9 Ultra.

B. The S9 FE is slower (though it is not that noticeable outside of games).

C. The FE has a 90Hz LCD display, while the Ultra has a 120Hz OLED screen.
